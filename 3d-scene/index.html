Uniot Platform provides an unparalleled environment for the execution of automated scripts on IoT devices. It is designed for DIY developers and businesses who aim to elevate their control over IoT devices and introduce a Web3 approach to their operations. By leveraging real-time monitoring, debugging, and complete customization of your device's functionality, Uniot offers a new level of interconnectivity and control for your devices.
